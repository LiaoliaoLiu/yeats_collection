<PoemSection>

# Though loud years come
- Though loud years come and loud years go
- A friend is the best thing here below.
- Shall we a better marvel find
- When the loud years have fallen behind?

</PoemSection>

<PoemSection>

# 尽管热闹岁月来
- 尽管热闹岁月来，热闹岁月去，
- 剩下最好的东西是一个朋友。
- 等热闹的岁月落到身后之时，
- 我们还可能找到更好的奇迹？

</PoemSection>
</PoemContent>
